# Edmonton MeshCore

Edmonton is the second regional MeshCore community within the Alberta MeshCore network.

Serving Alberta's capital region, Edmonton continues to grow a strong community of operators, hobbyists, emergency communicators, makers, and technology enthusiasts working together to expand resilient off-grid communications throughout Central Alberta.

The regional identifier used throughout Alberta MeshCore documentation and monitoring is:

`YEG`

---

## Building the Edmonton Network

The long-term vision is to expand observer stations, beacon nodes, dashboards and regional monitoring throughout the Edmonton metropolitan area while maintaining seamless interoperability with Calgary and the rest of Alberta.

Future documentation will include:

- Node recommendations
- Observer locations
- MQTT infrastructure
- Coverage maps
- Regional dashboards
- Local repeaters
- Installation guides
- Community projects

---

## Alberta MeshCore

Edmonton forms a key part of the Alberta MeshCore initiative, connecting communities across the province through shared infrastructure, open standards and collaborative development.

Together with Calgary and future regional deployments, Alberta MeshCore aims to build one of Canada's most comprehensive community-driven mesh communication networks.
